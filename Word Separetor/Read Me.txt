This simple C# program converts your text into individual words.
To change the text contents change the string 'userInput' (line 12).

Note: this program has the ability to skip any spaces, your text could include more than one space at a time and the program would know to skip it.

Example:

Input:
  There   are  a lot  of spaces   in this text

Output:
[1]There
[2]are
[3]a
[4]lot
[5]of
[6]spaces
[7]in
[8]this
[9]text
